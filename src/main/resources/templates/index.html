    The DrugBank database is a unique bioinformatics and cheminformatics resource that combines detailed drug data with comprehensive drug target information.

   The latest release of DrugBank (version 5.1.1, released 2018-07-03) contains 11,680 drug entries including 2,625 approved small molecule drugs, 1,115 approved biotech (protein/peptide) drugs, 128 nutraceuticals and over 5,504 experimental drugs. Additionally, 5,129 non-redundant protein (i.e. drug target/enzyme/transporter/carrier) sequences are linked to these drug entries. Each DrugCard entry contains more than 200 data fields with half of the information being devoted to drug/chemical data and the other half devoted to drug target or protein data.
